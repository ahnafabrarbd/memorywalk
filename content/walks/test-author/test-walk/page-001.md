The fence runs along the canal for about three hundred meters before the first gap appears. Someone cut the links with bolt cutters and bent them back to make a door-shaped opening. The edges have rusted into soft orange curls. Through it you can see the loading bay of a warehouse that closed in 2019.

The concrete is warm under clear November light. Everything is still.
